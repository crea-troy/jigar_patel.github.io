I'm a physicist and ML researcher driven by a specific discomfort: I don't like predictions without honest uncertainty. In physics, an overconfident algorithm can mask a new particle; in AI, it can mislead a decision. So I build things that are honest about what they don't know.

I grew up in India, spent four years teaching physics, then moved to Germany to join the research world at JGU Mainz — working on everything from detector hardware to rare B-meson decays at Belle II. I eventually found my home at the intersection of Bayesian deep learning and high-energy physics.

Currently a Research Assistant at INFN Padova with Prof. Tommaso Dorigo, working on physics-informed generative models for detector optimization as part of the MODE Collaboration. Actively seeking PhD and fellowship opportunities in Europe — MSCA Doctoral Networks, CERN, INFN-affiliated programs.

Featured Bayesian DL UQ Architecture
Precision-Weighted Confidence Propagation (PWCP)
2025 — Ongoing

A neural network architecture that propagates calibrated uncertainty in a single forward pass via Bayesian inverse-variance message passing, inspired by Friston's predictive coding. No Monte Carlo sampling — clean analytic uncertainty at standard-network speed. Achieves the lowest Expected Calibration Error across five baselines on MNIST and CIFAR-10. Now extending as a layer in Transformer-based LHC jet encoders on RODEM benchmarks.

Anomaly Detection Diffusion Models LHC Jets
Bayesian Latent Diffusion for Collider Anomaly Detection
2025 — 2026

Physics-informed anomaly detection for LHC jet data using a Bayesian latent diffusion model. Probabilistic encoder + latent-space diffusion with mass decorrelation and latent-correlation regularisation. Published on arXiv. Now extending to a Transformer+PWCP architecture incorporating event-level kinematics for per-event epistemic uncertainty on RODEM jet constituents.

SNN + Transformer IceCube Neutrino Physics
τ Neutrino Identification in IceCube · Bayesian SNN+Transformer
2025 — Ongoing

Hybrid Bayesian Spiking Neural Network–Transformer for real-time ντ classification in IceCube HESE data. SNN front-end encodes Cherenkov photon arrival times; Transformer integrates global spatial correlations. Per-event epistemic uncertainty via variational inference.
