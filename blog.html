Uncertainty in high-energy physics
What does it mean for a measurement to be uncertain? How do physicists and ML researchers think about this differently — and what can each learn from the other?

Predictive coding for physicists
Friston's framework is fascinating but usually explained in neuroscience terms. I want to translate it into the language of probabilistic graphical models for a physics audience.

The philosophy of anomaly detection
If you're looking for "something unusual" without knowing what it is, what does your algorithm actually represent? A meditation on model-independence and Bayesian surprise.

Physics infrastructure in India
India has extraordinary scientific talent but fragmented infrastructure. Some thoughts on what a world-class collider program in India could look like, and why it matters.
📊
Diffusion models from a physicist's eye
Score-based generative models have a beautiful connection to Langevin dynamics and statistical mechanics. Let's derive it from first principles.

Navigating academia as an outsider
From teaching physics in India to INFN Padova — what the path actually looks like, and what I wish I'd known earlier about how academic research works.
